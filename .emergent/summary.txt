- **Pocket Games:** A section for simple games like Chess, Tic Tac Toe, and Checkers, offering small coin rewards.
- **Pocket Tools:** A section for tools like a Calendar and Calculator.

- **Achievement System:** A new profile tab to display earned achievements.
- **Pets:** Animated pets that provide small bonuses (e.g., a mop pet for the Chore sector).
- **UI/UX:**
